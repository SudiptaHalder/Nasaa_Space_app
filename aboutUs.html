Our Team

Our team, Harvest Horizons, is a dedicated group of innovators from Bangladesh, united by our passion for utilizing advanced technology to address real-world challenges in agriculture. We are proud participants in the NASA International Space Apps Challenge 2024 with our project titled "Navigating Environmental Challenges in Agriculture." Comprising five members, our team is driven by a shared mission to empower farmers with cutting-edge solutions. With backgrounds in engineering, computer science, and geospatial analysis, we are working towards creating a tool that leverages NASA's Earth observation data to tackle critical agricultural issues such as water management, crop health monitoring, and sustainable farming practices. Our vision is to deliver a user-friendly platform that integrates real-time data with expert insights, helping farmers make informed decisions to optimize their yields and manage resources efficiently. Through this challenge, we aim to combine our technical skills and agricultural insights to contribute to the global farming community, providing them with practical solutions for managing water resources, preventing crop diseases, and improving agricultural resilience. Our commitment to innovation, collaboration, and sustainable development will drive our efforts to create impactful solutions for farmers worldwide.
